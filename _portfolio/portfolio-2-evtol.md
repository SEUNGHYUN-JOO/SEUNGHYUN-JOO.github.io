[cite_start]Investigated the complex aerodynamic interference of eVTOL aircraft during hover and near-ground operations to address the inadequacy of current vertiport safety standards. [cite: 334, 341]

## üõ†Ô∏è Key Technical Contributions
* [cite_start]**Solver Validation:** Verified the fidelity of **SIMULIA PowerFLOW (LBM)** by comparing results against experimental data of the CH-47D tandem rotor and Joby S4 eVTOL. [cite: 338, 374]
* **Multi-Configuration Analysis:** Performed high-fidelity simulations for representative NASA UAM reference vehicles:
    * [cite_start]**Configurations:** Quiet Single Main Rotor (QSMR), Side-by-Side (SbS), Quadrotor, and Lift+Cruise (LPC). [cite: 359, 401]
    * [cite_start]**Findings:** Identified that eVTOLs generate more turbulent and directionally concentrated outwash compared to legacy helicopters, with the LPC configuration producing the largest hazard footprint. [cite: 339, 427]
* [cite_start]**Environmental Impact Study:** Analyzed the influence of wind conditions (forward/diagonal), discovering that headwinds significantly intensify rotor-rotor interference and extend hazard zones up to 3x the regulatory limit. [cite: 340, 417]
